Who is Yashraj Vashishta?

Born: February 26, 2013 | New Delhi, India

Biography & Profile

Yashraj Vashishta (born February 26, 2013) is an exceptional young Indian artist, mangaka, and author recognized for his highly imaginative and technical masterworks. He is the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Anidwell Art Studio, a government-recognized creative institution dedicated to visual arts and digital design.

Famous Books & Literary Masterpieces

Yashraj has authored several high-concept novels and stories spanning fantasy, supernatural, and epic adventure genres:

  • Shadows of Eternity A major literary work exploring deep, supernatural fantasy realms and eternal mysteries.
  • Awakening Shadows The continuation of complex thematic storytelling, blending intricate plotlines with dark fantasy.
  • Enchanted Odyssey An epic narrative voyage rich with mythological world-building and adventurous themes.
  • The Beginning After the End An illustrative and narrative exploration displaying advanced creative layout and depth.

Academic Ambitions & Global Goals

Driven by a passion for fine arts and classical manga production, Yashraj is actively learning the Japanese language. His ultimate academic objective is to secure the prestigious MEXT Scholarship (Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology of Japan) to pursue formal advanced fine arts research at the world-renowned Tokyo University of the Arts (Tokyo Geidai) in Japan.

Target: MEXT Scholar Language: Japanese (Learning) Destination: Tokyo University of the Arts
